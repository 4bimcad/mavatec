We strive to provide our clients with maximum transparency and favorable terms. In this section you will find detailed information about our pricing and discount system.

Discount System

We offer discounts from 0.5% to 5% on gold and silver purchases, depending on order volume and current market conditions. Our goal is to provide you with the best possible conditions for investing and buying metals.

Monthly Price Update

Every month we publish current gold prices and available discounts. This information includes:

  • Available Quantity of Gold: Indicates the amount of gold we have available for sale.
  • Current Discount: The level of discount we can offer this month.
This data will be updated monthly and you will receive it directly to your email. We strive to be as transparent as possible about pricing and discounts so that you can plan your investments in a timely manner.

How ​​it works

  • 1. Price Determination: Our specialists analyze market conditions and determine the optimal price for gold, taking into account current economic factors.
  • 2. Calculation of Discounts: Depending on the volume of purchase and the market situation, we set discounts that can vary from 0.5% to 5%.
  • 3. Update and Newsletter: At the beginning of each month, we update the prices and discounts on our website and send a newsletter to all our customers. In the letter you will find:
    • - Current gold prices.
    • - Available amount of gold.
    • - Applicable discounts for the current month.

Advantages

  • - Transparency: You will always be aware of current prices and available discounts.
  • - Flexibility: We offer different levels of discounts to suit the needs of both small and large investors.
  • - Convenience: Regular updates and mailings will help you not to miss profitable offers and make investments in a timely manner.
If you have any questions or require additional information about prices and discounts, do not hesitate to contact our support team. We are always ready to help you with any questions and provide you with all the necessary information.
