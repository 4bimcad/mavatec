Silver buying process


(Same process as buying gold)

1. KYC check
- To start purchasing silver, the client must pass the KYC (Know Your Customer) check. Fill out and submit the KYC form in advance to identify and verify your identity.

2. Request to buy silver
- After completing KYC, the client must send an email with a request to purchase silver. The letter must indicate the amount of silver you want to purchase and your contact information.

3. Filling out data for the contract
- After receiving the request, we send the client a link to fill out the data to form a forward contract. Please fill out all fields carefully and submit the data to create a contract.

4. Signing the contract
- The client must sign a forward contract to purchase silver. The signed contract must be sent to us to confirm the relations.

5. Transfer of funds
- After signing the contract, the client manually transfers funds to the company’s account. Payment details will be provided in the contract.

6. Production and supply of metal
- After receiving funds, the company begins the process of producing silver. We guarantee that the metal will be produced and delivered within the time period specified in the contract.

7. Getting silver
- The client picks up the metal at the specified location. At the time of receipt, the client will be provided with all related documents and certificates confirming the authenticity and quality of the silver.

Notes
- The client can resell his contract to another verified client of the company. This is written in detail in the contract. Our company acts as an intermediary and guarantor of the transaction.
- On the date of execution of the contract, the Client has the right to receive the equivalent in money for the purchased silver at the current market price.



Benefits of buying Silver from us:

- Security and confidentiality: All client data is securely protected and is not transferred to third parties.

- Quality Guarantee: We provide certificates for each batch of silver confirming its quality and authenticity.

- Transparency of the transaction: Every stage of the purchasing process is transparent and understandable, which ensures trust and confidence in our services.

- ALWAYS Cheaper than others: We are producers of silver and therefore can sell it at a discount. By concluding a forward contract with us, you help us produce silver, and we share the profit with you.

We strive to establish reliable and long-term business relationships with each client. Your trust and satisfaction are our priority.
